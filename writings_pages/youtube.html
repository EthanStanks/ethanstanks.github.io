Analyzing YouTube Video Engagement

Description

In my analysis paper, I examine viewer engagement on YouTube, concentrating on MrBeast's channels. By employing data scraping, I gathered and analyzed engagement metrics to identify the factors influencing viewer interactions. This analysis offers a detailed understanding of content engagement on YouTube, providing practical insights for content creators aiming to enhance their strategies and audience engagement.
