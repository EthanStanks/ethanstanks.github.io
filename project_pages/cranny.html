Description

In this two-month project, I played a pivotal role in developing Cranny, an Android Studio Kotlin app. I focused on creating the social page, including a feed to showcase friends' recently read books and profile information. Additionally, I implemented Firebase's Google authentication for the login page and created classes for CRUD operations on Firebase.

Details

  • Social Media Development
  • Firebase's Google authentication
  • CRUD System
  • Debug Support for Team Members
